Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, cheese, meat sauce, and sometimes vegetables. To make lasagna, you will need lasagna noodles, ground beef or sausage, tomato sauce, ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, and seasonings like garlic, salt, and pepper.

To start, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions and set them aside. Brown the ground beef or sausage in a pan and add tomato sauce and seasonings. In a separate bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, and more seasonings. Now it's time to assemble the lasagna. Spread a layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish, followed by a layer of noodles, a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture, and a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat these layers until you have used up all of your ingredients, ending with a layer of mozzarella cheese on top. Bake in the oven at 375°F for about 30-40 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Dried basil
  • Dried oregano
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. Brown ground beef or sausage in a large skillet over medium heat. Drain off any excess fat.
  4. Add chopped onion and garlic to the skillet and cook until softened.
  5. Pour in a jar of tomato sauce and stir to combine with the meat mixture. Season with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning to taste.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix together ricotta cheese, egg, grated Parmesan cheese, and chopped fresh parsley.
  7. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  9. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  10. Repeat layers of sauce, noodles, and ricotta cheese mixture until all ingredients are used up, ending with a layer of sauce on top.
  11. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the top of the lasagna.
  12. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the lasagna is heated through.
  14. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
